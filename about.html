About me

Hi, I’m Jernej, a UI designer with a background in Media studies.
I acquired a profound knowledge of photography, videography, and mass media during my studies, which gave me a good sense of aesthetics and human behaviour.
After my studies, I’ve lived in four different countries and learned to speak four languages, which gave me a broad viewpoint on other cultures and habits.
My interest in technology, innovations, and people has led me to jump into the world of User Interface design, where I can combine all of my
skills, experiences, and passion for creating aesthetically pleasing designs that people love to use.
